Development Log

Tracking our journey from prototype to production.

Current Update • Software

Vision-Language Model Optimization

We are currently optimizing OCRNet to better handle complex PDF table recognition. The challenge lies in running these heavy models on the Jetson Orin Nano's embedded hardware while maintaining real-time latency. Early tests show a 20% speed improvement.

November 2023 • Hardware

Prototype Housing Design

We have finalized the CAD designs for the 20-cell refreshable display unit. We are utilizing 3D printing for the initial casing to test ergonomics and heat dissipation from the Orin Nano.

October 2023 • Integration

Speech Pipeline Success

Successfully tested NVIDIA Riva integration. We can now convert spoken audio to text, process it through our Python logic module, and output Braille signals with minimal latency.
